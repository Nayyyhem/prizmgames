Realm Stories

Welcome to Realm Stories, a narrative adventure game where you are the Chief of a medieval fantasy town.

Your town is full of colorful and quirky characters, each with their own stories to tell. As the Chief, you will see their stories play out on your screen, and make choices that will affect their lives and the future of your town.
